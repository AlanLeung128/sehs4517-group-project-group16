About Us

Welcome to Cerebral Corner! We provide a comfortable board game environment, and when you're feeling sleepy, grab a cup of coffee to perk you up! Whether you're a seasoned player or a newbie just starting out with board games, you're sure to find your enjoyment here.

Best Game Of The Month

Avalon

"Embark on a magnificent adventure of wit and deception!"
Want to experience a true brainstorming session? Avalon is the perfect choice! In this game, you will become a Knight of the Round Table under King Arthur, but beware—a traitor has infiltrated your ranks. Will you be Merlin, who knows everything yet must remain silent, guiding your companions to victory with the most subtle hints? Or will you be a cunning henchman feigning loyalty, delivering a fatal blow at the most crucial moment?

Brand Story

Cerebral: Refers to "intellectual and mental." It symbolizes that every roll of the dice, every card game, is a pure mental storm, a clash of logic and inspiration.
Corner: Meaning "corner." It represents a gentle haven in the hustle and bustle of the city, providing a comfortable space for undisturbed solitude or gatherings with close friends.
The story begins on an ordinary afternoon, where, outside the fast-paced reality, we discover that people seem to lack a sanctuary where their brains can slow down.
